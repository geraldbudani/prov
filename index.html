About Me

I am Klea Uruci, a second-year Architecture student at Epoka University in Tirana. I have a strong passion for sustainable design and a keen eye for detail, which I integrate into my coursework. Currently, I am seeking an internship opportunity to apply my skills and further develop my knowledge in a practical, real-world setting.
